# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Friend groups, colleagues, and families making casual group decisions — primarily on mobile.
Context: social settings: at a table, in a chat thread, on-the-go.
Primary task: create a quick vote and share it, or cast a vote someone sent you.
Secondary users: German-speaking and English-speaking audiences in equal measure.

## Product Purpose
entscheid removes friction from group decision-making: no account, no app install, no drama.
One person creates a vote, shares a link, everyone votes, the result appears live.
Success = a group makes a decision faster than they would by chatting about it.

## Brand Personality
Decisive. Friendly. Zero-friction.

## Anti-references
- Doodle (too feature-heavy, dated UI)
- Generic dark SaaS UIs with purple gradient accents and glowing cards
- Survey tools (too formal, too long)
- Any UI where the design feels like it was generated rather than crafted

## Design Principles
1. Speed over features — every screen completable in under 10 seconds
2. The interface disappears into the task — chrome is minimal, content is king
3. Bilingual without compromise — German and English feel equally native, not translated
4. Earned delight — animation and color serve function, never decoration
5. Trust through simplicity — no accounts, no data hunger, nothing suspicious

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Support prefers-reduced-motion. Touch targets ≥ 44px.
Both German and English UI copy must feel natural, not machine-translated.
